If you run a small business, you probably answer the same messages over and over. "Do you have it in stock?", "How much does it cost?", "Are you open tomorrow?"

A chatbot does that for you. It doesn't replace anyone — it frees your team from repetitive tasks so they can focus on what matters.

## How It Works in Practice

Imagine someone messages your WhatsApp at 11 PM asking about a product. Without a chatbot, that message sleeps until the next day. With a chatbot, they get an instant response with the price, availability, and a purchase link.

That person won't go looking for you elsewhere. You're closing the sale while you sleep.

## What a Chatbot Can Do Today

- Answer frequently asked questions instantly
- Qualify leads: separate the ones ready to buy from the ones just browsing
- Schedule appointments and reservations without touching a calendar
- Send payment links, catalogs, and locations
- Hand off to a human when the query needs it

## Is It Expensive?

Five years ago, setting up a chatbot cost thousands of dollars and required developers. Today, tools like the ones we use at Tp3studio let you have one up and running in 48 hours for less than what you spend on monthly advertising.

## It's Not About Technology

It's about making sure your business doesn't disappear when you're not available. Your customers want answers now. The chatbot is the bridge between their urgency and your ability to respond.

If you can order food delivery, you can have a chatbot. Seriously.
